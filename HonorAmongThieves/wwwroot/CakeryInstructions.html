INTRODUCTION

You are professional thief. Or at least, you're trying to be one.

OBJECTIVE

The objective of this game is to make the most money from selling baked goods.
This is cumulative - your total score at the end is the sum of the money you earnt from selling your goods every round.
Your score is based off the total sales, not profit.

MECHANICS

Every round, you get to buy ingredients, or create them via other means.
Using these ingredients, you bake Cookies, Croissants, or Cakes.
At the end of every round, all your Cookies, Cakes, and Croissants are sold in the market.
In general, the more of one good is in the market, the less they will sell for.
The prices of each good will also fluctuate from round to round, and from one game to the next.
