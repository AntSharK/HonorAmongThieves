INTRODUCTION

You've done it. At long last, you have opened your very own bakery.
But what's this? It appears that many other bakeries have opened right across the street. At the exact same time!
This cannot stand. You must be the greatest baker of all.

OBJECTIVE

The objective of this game is to make the most money from selling baked goods.
This is cumulative - your total score at the end is the sum of the money you earn from selling your goods every round.
Your score is based off your total sales, not profit.

MECHANICS


UPGRADES

You start the game with a certain amount of "Upgrade Credit". This can only be used for buying upgrades. When you run out of "Upgrade Credit", you will use money to buy upgrades. Upgrades purchased can only be used in the next round.
Some upgrades have a limited number of uses - purchase more to get more uses.
Some upgrades have an unlimited number of uses - purchase more to make them more efficient.

INGREDIENTS

Every round, you get to buy ingredients, or create them via upgrades you own. Ingredients are used to bake Cookies, Croissants, or Cakes.
Unused ingredients at the end of every round are refunded at a discount.

BAKING

Using ingredients (or upgrades), you bake Cookies, Croissants, or Cakes. At the end of every round, all baked goods are automatically sold.
In general, the more of one good is in the market, the less they will sell for. The selling price of each good will also fluctuate from round to round.

SETUP

Each player starts with an 'Upgrade Credit'. This is an amount of money that can only be used for upgrading.
Each player gets an 'Annual Allowance' at the start of every year.
