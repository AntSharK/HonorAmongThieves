INTRODUCTION

You are professional thief. Or at least, you're trying to be one.
You wouldn't be one if the job market wasn't this terrible.
Fortunately, your parents have granted you a sizable fortune to live off.
This fortune also paid for your 2 masters degrees in social work and fine art. Which might explain your job situation.

OBJECTIVE

The objective of this game is to be the richest thief alive by the time you retire.
The game begins in 2018 and lasts an arbitrary number of years. At the end, everyone retires together.
The upper limit for the number of years the game lasts is set by the room creator.
The lower limit for the number of years the game lasts is half of the upper limit (rounded down).

HEIST OCCURRENCE

Every year, you will take part in a heist.
For everyone's security, your heist mates are chosen by an arbitrary third party.
Each heist consists of 2 or more thieves (including you). The upper bound is set by the room creator.
The reward for a heist increases the larger it is (i.e. more thieves equals more money per person).

GOING ON HEISTS

You will be given a choice of whether or not to take part in the heist.
If half (rounded up) or more of the thieves in the heist choose to take part, the heist will occur.
The total reward is always the same. So the fewer thieves take part, the larger the reward per thief.
If more than half of the thieves don't show up, the heist does not happen and no one earns anything for that year.

SNITCHING

You can also opt to snitch on your fellow thieves.
If you snitch on them, and the heist happens, then every single thief that went on the heist goes to jail, and you get reward money.
Snitching on a heist also means that the heist does not dish out any reward.
If you snitch on them, and the heist doesn't happen, you get put in jail for one year.
If multiple thieves snitch, the reward money is split between them.
Note that you can snitch on a heist you take part in.

GOING TO JAIL

If you are sent to jail because you took part in a heist, your next jail sentence will be twice as long.
If you are sent to jail because you snitched on a heist that did not happen, you will always be sentenced for only one year. Your sentence will not increase.
If you are sent to jail on a heist that you snitched on, you will receive the minimum sentence. But your next sentence will still increase.

COMMITTING MURDER

You can also opt to murder a thief in the same heist as you.
This option makes you go on the heist.
If the other thief does not show up for the heist, then you don't try to kill him.
In the event that the other thief has snitched on a heist that occurred, you kill him.
Killing another thief will give you his entire fortune and remove that thief from play.
However, if you try to kill a thief who has never snitched on a heist that occurred, you die and lose the game.
If multiple players successfully kill a thief, his fortune is split evenly.

SPECIAL CONDITIONS

If only 1 thief is out of jail and alive, then no heists happen that year.
If everyone dies, then there is no winner and you should also stop hanging out with this group of people.
